Welcome to Task Management System

Are you here for personal use or for your company?

Welcome to the Task Management System! As a personal user, you have access to various features to help you stay organized. Login to your account using your username and password on the login page. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your personal user dashboard. From the dashboard, you can create tasks, add to-do items, and keep track of your schedule. To create a task, click on the "Create Task" button and provide the necessary details such as task name, description, and due date. You can also add to-do items by clicking on the "Add To-Do" button and specifying the task, description, and due date for each item. Use the dashboard to manage and update your tasks and to-dos as needed.
