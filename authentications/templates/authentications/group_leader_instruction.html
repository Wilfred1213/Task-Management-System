Welcome to Task Management System

Are you here for personal use or for your company?

Hello, group lead! As a leader, you have additional capabilities to manage projects and assign tasks to group members. Login to your account using your username and password on the login page. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your group lead dashboard. From the dashboard, you can create a group by providing a group name and inviting members to sign up for the Task Management System. After your group members have signed up, you can create projects and assign tasks to specific group members. To create a project, navigate to the "Projects" section and click on the "Create Project" button. Specify the project details such as name, description, and due date. Once the project is created, you can assign tasks to group members by selecting the project and choosing the member to assign the task to. The assigned tasks will appear in the respective group members' dashboards.
