Welcome, group member! As a member of a group, you will receive task assignments and can keep track of your to-do list. Login to your account using your username and password on the login page. Once logged in, you will be redirected to your group member dashboard. From the dashboard, you can view the tasks assigned to you by the group lead. Additionally, you can create your own to-do list or check your email for any task assignments sent by the group lead. To create a personal to-do item, click on the "Add To-Do" button and provide the task details such as name, description, and due date. Use the dashboard to manage and update your personal to-do list.
